## Species Relations 

**Institutionalized racism** between humans, diaphanic humans, and synthetics does not exist in the years following the Great War. Humans, Diaphanic Humans, and Synthetics share equal positions in society throughout post-war Eris. 

That being said, relationships between the three humanoid species are both nuanced and unpredictable.

### Humans and Diaphanic Humans 

As diaphany occurs at a rate of 1 in 8 births, diaphanic humans have become commonplace in Shenjiang. After the Great War, the life expectancy of diaphanic humans has increased significantly from 21 years of age to 42 years of age, due to improved living conditions and access to medical care. It is not uncommon for some siblings in the same family to show signs of diaphany, while others remain healthy. 

Diaphanic humans are generally regarded with pity, while others may fear the powers that diaphanic humans are capable of. However, due to the role that diaphany played in winning the Great War, as well as the diaphanic status of Shenjiang's Prime Minister, diaphanic humans maintain relatively normal lives in this society.
