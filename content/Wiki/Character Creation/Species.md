Below is a list of playable species and relevant player information. 
## Humans

Humans have populated the likes of Eris for thousands of years. Created in Goddess Vega's likeness, they are–for better or for worse–versatile, durable, and clever. 

### Diaphanic 

While direct and repeated contact with Lunacite typically leads to fatal illness, indirect exposure to Lunacite has  been traced to the births of children with a condition termed *diaphany*. Diaphanic humans have access to the supernatural properties of Lunacite, but at the cost of their health and constitution. In player terms, diaphanic humans have access to one DND cantrip, usually at the cost of health points or constitution score. 

 Note that diaphanic humans are **born** with diaphany. They cannot **contract** diaphany through contact with Lunacite or another human. 

Physical symptoms of diaphany can be identified through gray-black skin on parts of one's limbs, hands, or body, with superficial "cracks" that emit a golden light. Diaphanic lesions typically cluster at one point–it is more common to see diaphanic individuals with blackened lesions on the tips of their fingers, parts of their face, or on their chest, but not in all three areas at the same time. 

For more on society's view of diaphany and the Diaphanic at large, see [[Society]].
